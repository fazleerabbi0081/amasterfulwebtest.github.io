Dear Colleagues
A warm welcome to the first international conference- Innovations in Food Science & Technology, to be held in Erding, Munich Germany from 10-17 May 2017.
 
You are invited to participate in the conference and present and share your research with international researchers and leaders in the area of Food Science & Technology.
 
Join the leading experts and present your research in the vibrant city of Munich.
 
We look forward to see you in Munich!

Conference Chairs
Submit Your Abstract

You are invited to submit and present your research at this international conference with global audience in the area of Food Science & Technology. Oral and poster abstracts are invited on the following themes: .
1. Novel Food Processing Technologies
2. Food Process Engineering
3. Food Chemistry and Analysis
4. Food Product Technologies
5. Food Microbiology
6. Functional Foods
.
Young Scientist Poster Award


The scientific committee of the conference invites abstracts for a Young Scientist Best Poster Award. In case your submitted abstract is accepted for a presentation in the poster sessions, the quality of the work, the layout of the poster, and performance of your presentation will be assessed by the scientific committee members, and the best three posters will be awarded as follows: .

First Prize: €500  

   

Second Prize: €300 

            

Third Prize: €200

Eligiblity – B.Sc., M.Sc., Ph.D. Candidates

Applicant should NOT be over 29 years of age at the start of the academic year.
