Welcome To Conference
A warm welcome to the first international conference- Innovations in Food Science & Technology, to be held in Erding, Munich, Germany from 10-17 May 2017.
 
You are invited to participate in the conference and present and share your research with international researchers and leaders in the area of Food Science & Technology.
 
Join the leading experts and present your research in the vibrant city of Munich.
 
We look forward to see you in Munich!
